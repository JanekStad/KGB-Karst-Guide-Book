First Ascent

Dave Graham, 2005

Safety

PG-13 (Bad Landing)

Start low on the obvious jug rail. Move right to the sharp crimp, generate huge momentum for the dyno to the lip. The crux is holding the swing. A classic test piece for the grade requiring serious finger strength and core tension.

Warning: The landing is uneven, multiple pads and spotters recommended.
